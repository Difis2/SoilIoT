About

In today's world, we face many challenges when it comes to food production.

Growing Population

Increases, not only the demand for agricultural product, but also for urban expansion, which leads to a decrease of agricultural fields, and consequently, a decrease in agricultural production.

Climate Changes

One of the biggest challenges and threat to the human health and agriculture. It is defined as significant changes in the average values of weather elements like precipitation and temperature.

Wastage of resources

The amount of waste of resources duo to unefficient agriculture methods should be reduced. This waste is being reduced with Precision Agriculture, from a perspective of resources and nutrient use, efficiency, and ecological impact.

Objectives

Main objective: Development of an IoT ecosystem to measure parameters and to control the water and nutrient concentration.

Develop an IoT ecosystem that includes measurement nodes of soil characteristics, and computing and communication platforms based on type microcontrollers ESP32.

Develop a management software component including geographic information on the location of nodes, data recording and graphical representation of the evolution of soil parameters: a mobile application to control, read and graphical representation.

Develop a server-level data analysis module.
